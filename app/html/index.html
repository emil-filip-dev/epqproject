Welcome to the index of my EPQ Project website!

The purpose of this website is to offer an easy introduction to fusion energy.

Understanding the basics of fusion energy is important, as it could become humanity's main source of energy in the future.

The information is intended to be read tab-by-tab and page-by-page.

A minimum GCSE-grade level of knowledge is recommended, but some topics are much easier to understand than others. Feel free to go at your own pace!

A large number of animations and videos are provided to ease understanding. Check out the Further Reading tab for more resources and the links for the videos and animations!

If you're ready, try out the Quiz and see how high you can score!

Have fun!
